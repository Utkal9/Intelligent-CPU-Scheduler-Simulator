About the Simulator

The Intelligent CPU Scheduler Simulator is a comprehensive tool designed to help users visualize and compare various CPU scheduling algorithms in real-time. With features such as detailed performance metrics, Gantt chart visualizations, and interactive inputs, this simulator provides an intuitive learning experience for students, professionals, and educators.

Whether you're analyzing the efficiency of First Come First Serve, Shortest Job First, Round Robin, or Priority Scheduling algorithms, this platform ensures accurate results and a seamless simulation experience. Our goal is to bridge the gap between theory and practical understanding of CPU scheduling.
